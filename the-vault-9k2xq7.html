The Lore

The gallery no one tended

Cubist Souls · est. by its holders

Ten thousand cubist portraits once hung in a gallery. Then the maker walked away — the lights went out, the frames gathered dust, and the collection was left to fade.

But the paintings were never truly abandoned. They were waiting for the people who still loved them. One by one, the community came back in, recovered the art and the names, and hung every soul back on the wall — this time, it belongs to us.

Cubist Souls is the first collection freed and rebuilt by its own community. Not flipped. Not left behind again. Reclaimed.

The rite · Room II

How a soul is freed

You don't buy a Cubist Soul. You free one — by burning the old canvas that trapped it. The soul keeps its number and its face, and comes back on a contract we own.

Act I

Choose the canvas

Pick one — or your whole wallet. You only approve the exact pieces you select, or all of them in one signature for big holders.

Act II

Light the fire

Burning destroys the Pikkazo forever on Ethereum. No undo, no refund. An irreversible act of liberation.

Act III

Free the soul

Its Cubist Soul — same number, original art recovered — is minted straight to your wallet. No fee beyond gas.

Built on a Diamond. Cubist Souls runs on a modular contract (EIP-2535) — one that grows instead of freezing. New rooms open forever: traits, staking, lore, on-chain mechanics — all without ever migrating again. Same collection, same token IDs. This is the final address; we only build on top.
